Welcome to Jurmala, the Baltic Beauty!

Ah, Jurmala, the gem of Latvia's coast! Imagine a town where picturesque wooden houses meet ornate Art Nouveau architecture, where the sound of the Baltic Sea waves gently caresses your ears, and where the locals are as friendly as can be. You're in for a treat, folks!

Fun Facts about Jurmala

Before we dive into the good stuff, let's get acquainted with some amusing tidbits about this lovely town:

  • The Longest Beach: Jurmala boasts the longest beach in the Baltic States, stretching for a whopping 26 kilometers!
  • Wooden Heritage: The town is home to over 4,000 wooden houses, many of which date back to the 19th and early 20th centuries. Talk about charm!
  • Health Resort Heritage: Jurmala has been a popular health resort since the 18th century, thanks to its therapeutic mud and mineral-rich water. Spa lovers, rejoice!
