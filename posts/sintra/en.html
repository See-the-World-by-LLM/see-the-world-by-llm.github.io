Fun Facts About Sintra

  • A Town of Many Castles: Boasting an impressive collection of palaces and castles, Sintra is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. From the colorful Pena Palace to the mysterious Quinta da Regaleira, you'll feel like you're in a real-life game of thrones.
  • Literary Inspiration: Sintra's beauty has inspired some of the world's most renowned writers, including Lord Byron, who famously penned "Cain" while soaking up the town's dramatic atmosphere.
