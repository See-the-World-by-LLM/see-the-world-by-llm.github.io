Local Food You Must Try (Or Risk Regretting Forever)

Your taste buds are about to thank you. Al Khaburah serves up Omani classics with a coastal twist. Don't leave without trying:

It‚Äôs a symphony of flavor, often topped with fried onions and nuts. Pro tip: Eat with your right hand (if you're comfortable) for maximum authenticity points and messy fingers.
  • Fresh Fish, Grilled to Perfection: Head to the fish market early in the morning (see itinerary!). Pick your favorite catch ‚Äì Kingfish, Hamour (grouper), or whatever looks freshest.   • Omani Halwa: Forget wobbly Western pudding. Omani Halwa is a dense, sticky, gelatinous confection made from sugar, rosewater, saffron, nuts, and ghee. It‚Äôs tender beyond belief and infused with incredible smoky, earthy flavors.
