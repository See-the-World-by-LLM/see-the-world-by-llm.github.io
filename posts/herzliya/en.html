Fun Facts About Herzliya

Before we dive into the good stuff, let's get to know Herzliya a little better. Here are three fun facts about this Israeli coastal city:

  • Herzliya has a rich history: The city was founded in 1924 and named after Theodor Herzl, the father of modern Zionism. Today, you can still visit the Herzl Museum to learn more about the history of the city and its namesake.
  • It's a popular celeb spot: Herzliya is a favorite among Israeli celebrities, who often retreat to the city's luxurious villas and private beaches to escape the paparazzi. Who knows, you might even spot a famous face or two while you're there!
  • The beaches are seriously stunning: With 13 kilometers of coastline, Herzliya boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in Israel. From hidden coves to bustling promenades, there's a beach to suit every taste.
